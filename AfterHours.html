Special Events

These menus are designed for happy hour or light reception events. Our culinary creativity and presentation will wow your employees and your clients alike. 25 person minimum. Call Terri at 913.262.2816 or send her an email at terri@decocateringkc.com to place your order today.

Gourmet Antipasto Display -

This is a client favorite. Italian meats, assorted cheeses, fresh olives, peppercinos, and assorted crackers make this platter as beautiful as it is delicious.

Dips, dips, dips

Our house made spinach dip, fresh hummus, and white bean dip. Served with tortilla chips, pita, and veggies.

Passed Hors D' Ourves

Our bacon wrapped, gouda stuffed dates, house made meatball sliders, spring rolls with peanut dipping sauce, shaved beef crostini, and a chefs choice seasonal option. All passed by our professional, uniformed servers. This menu is great for a networking event so that you don't have people standing around a buffet.
