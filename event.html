Chef-manned stations

Some stations can be an entire meal for your party, others are designed to be combined with other menu items to add flair and fun to the party.

Asian Stir Fry

Chef prepares your favorite stir fry. Choose from Chicken, beef, shrimp, a variety of vegetables and sauces. Served or sesame noodles.

Kansas City Barbecue Station

Brisket, Pulled Pork, or Chicken served on mini sandwiches. Mac n' cheese muffins, chorizo & molasses baked beans, and Asian or traditional coleslaw

Sausage Tasting Station

Locally Made Sausage display with gourmet mustards, cheeses, & assorted crackers.

Bruschetta Bar

Rustic bread, thickly sliced and crisped in extra virgin olive oil. Guests may choose diced tomatoes with Buffalo mozzarella; Sicilian caponata; chevre with sundried tomatoes and basil pesto

Risotto Station

Arborio rice cooked on-site by our professional chefs. Guests may select asparagus tips, butternut squash or shrimp. The risottos are finished with freshly ground black pepper and grated pecorino romano.

Smashed Potato Bar

Smashed potatoes with guests choice of herb garlic gravy, curry gravy, or homestyle gravy. Served in martini glasses & guests can top off with butter, sour cream, cream cheese, crumbled bacon, and chives.

Southwestern & Mediterranean Dipping Stations

Southwestern black bean dip, lemon garlic hummus, and 3 other seasonal dips. Fresh breads, crackers, and veggies available for dipping.

Gourmet Sandwich Station

Premium meats & Cheeses with fresh sauces & Gourmet condiments, Farm-to-Market breads

Our culinary team can also create custom menus just for your wedding or special event! Please inquire.

Prices fluctuate with the market & other factors. Please call or email us for up to date prices.
