Total Event Coordination, Amazing Food, Unmatched Service


Your wedding day is a very special day, and your catering experience for the day should be too. Deco will work with you to create a meal that looks as good as it tastes. Our presentation is clean, modern, and sleek. We have many unique options including our chef manned action stations, fun appetizers, and sleek, modern buffets. We work closely with each of our couples, leaving no detail of the reception untouched.

Regardless of menu selection, your reception will be staffed with a team of professional, uniformed servers who will help ensure your day is as perfect as you've always dreamed it would be. Look over our menus, but keep in mind that we can also prepare a custom menu to fit your individual tastes, budget, and style.
