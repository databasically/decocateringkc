Service

You will receive 6 free hours of consultation & event coordination services from our owners to ensure no detail is left untouched. A 19% service charge covers logistics, overhead, & other house costs. This is not a gratuity.

Deco's team is always professional & strives for perfection on every single event. We have no full time servers. Our servers work with us in their own time because they love the work & making our clients happy. Our staff all comes to us through referrals & our clients are always impressed.

Cake Cutting - $1.50 per guest

We will set up the cake table with a linen color of your choosing & tea lights. We will also provide a bottle of champagne, toasting glasses for the bride & groom, cake knife & server, plastic plates, forks, & napkins. Our professional servers will serve the cake.

Coffee & Other N/A Drink Service - $1.00-2.00 per guest

Let us know what you'd like to serve for an exact price. You can do these services for a percentage of your guests (ex: Coffee service for 50 on a guest count of 200).

Bar Service -

Deco can provide bartenders for your event for $30 per hour along with all barware rentals, linens, tables, ice, mixers, etc. You may set up your full bar through our bartenders or provide your own alcohol.
