Lunch
Our gourmet box lunches are a hit for meetings, training events, and team building activities. Please place box lunch orders by 11am the day before service is requested. Soup orders must be received 3 days prior to service. Due to the freshness and quality of the ingredients in our gourmet box lunches, we require a minimum order of 25 people & a minimum of 10 per item. Call us at 913.262.2816 or send an email to terri@decocateringkc.com to place your order today.
